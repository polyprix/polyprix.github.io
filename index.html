POLY PRIX

The low-poly competitive racing game inspired by Formula motorsport. Online races, leaderboards, custom liveries, and chaotic fun.

Follow Development

Game Modes

Race in online lobbies, compete in full seasons, practice for cash, or play solo with AI. Everyone uses the same balanced car — skill is the only advantage.

Customization

Create your identity: custom liveries, driver suits, and helmets. Stand out on track with your own unique style.

Low-Poly Tracks

Race around original parodies of real circuits from the 2025 calendar. Sauna, Gulfstone, Stormbar, Canyon City, Iron Desert — and more to come.
